# Trust Center

## How Scoring Works
- Review outputs include profile version, confidence context, evidence items, and provenance notes.
- Similarity and AI-pattern values are risk estimates for triage and quality control.

## Source Verification Visibility
- External checks report provider status, live-check capability, retention-awareness, and match evidence.

## Data Handling Posture
- Review artifacts are scoped to account and workspace context in the app.
- Processing may use configured backend and AI services.

## Export Auditability
- PDF exports include evidence ledger, scoring context, and provenance summaries.

## Version Transparency
- Scoring behavior is tied to profile/version identifiers to support reproducibility across releases.
